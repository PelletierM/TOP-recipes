Delicious Waffles

Description

This easy batter recipe is a great way to treat yourself with some delicious waffles in the morning (or any other time of the day!)

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2/3 cup salted butter
  • 4 egg whites
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Melt butter and let it rest at room temperature while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt and sugar) in a bowl.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk milk and egg yolks together.
  4. Add half of the dry ingredients in the large bowl and whisk batter until uniform.
  5. While continuously whisking, slowly add the melted butter to the batter.
  6. Add the remaining dry ingredients to the batter and whisk until uniform.
  7. Whisk egg whites until fluffy and fold them gently into the batter.
  8. Bake for about 5 minutes with your favorite waffle maker.
  9. Enjoy (with a ton of whipped cream)!
