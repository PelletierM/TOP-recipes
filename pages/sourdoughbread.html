Sourdough Bread

Description

This sourdough bread recipe creates a beautiful loaf with deep flavors and a mild tang. It is separated into 3 sessions, each separated by a couple hours of waiting to ensure that the dough has proofed properly.

Ingredients

  • 200 g sourdough starter 1
  • 400 ml water
  • 600 g bread flour
  • 10 g fine sea salt

1 If you do not already have one, you can use this recipe to make your own sourdough starter!

Steps

Session 1

  1. In a bowl, mix the sourdough starter with the water.
  2. Put the flour in a large bowl, making a well in the center, and add the starter and water.
  3. Mix the ingredients until it becomes a rough dough, and let it rest for about 45 minutes. Make sure that the bowl is covered, either with a towel or with plastic wrap.
  4. Take the dough out of the bowl and put it on a lightly floured surface. Add salt to the dough and knead it for about 10 minutes or until it's uniform and stretchy.
  5. Put the dough back in a large bowl and let it rest for 4 hours. Again, make sure that the bowl is covered.

Session 2

  1. Take the dough out of the bowl and put it on a lightly floured surface. Knead it for about 10 minutes.
  2. Form the dough in the shape of a ball and cover it with plastic wrap. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove the plastic wrap, and gently stretch the dough and fold it on itself from bottom to top.
  4. Turn the dough 90 degrees and repeat the previous step. Do this 4 times in a row.
  5. Without kneading it, form and stretch the dough in the shape of a ball.
  6. Place the dough inside a proofing basket, with the seam facing upwards. Cover the basket with a towel and put it inside a plastic bag, so that the dough doesnt dry while it's proofing.
  7. Put the dough inside the fridge and let it rest for 8 to 12 hours. The timing of this works best when you let the dough rest overnight.

Session 3

  1. Take the dough out of the fridge and let it rest at room temperature for about 1 hour.
  2. Put Dutch oven (with lid on) inside your oven and preheat to 450 F.
  3. Once the oven has reached its temperature, put the dough inside the Dutch oven and scar the dough. Cover with lid and bake in the oven for 50 minutes.
  4. Remove bread from the oven and let it rest on a cooling grid for 15 minutes.
  5. ENJOY!
